Arj Company:

Araj Factory is a home appliance manufacturer in Iran. This company has been engaged in production since 1316 and with a history of 124 years, it is the oldest manufacturer of household appliances in Iran and the Middle East. By 1357, Araj increased the number of its employees to nearly three thousand people and became the largest industrial factory in the Middle East. After the revolution of 1357, during the confiscation of property and assets, this factory belonging to the Arjmand family, who were Baha'is, was transferred to the National Industries Organization of Iran due to debt. Tomani also faced and after other problems, such as not updating the factory, on June 24, 1395, the 79-year-old factory of this company was officially closed with the judicial seal of Anbar Araj. Now, after about 40 years, Araj factory has returned to the first level of industrial factories in the world.
